Project Description

This project was a creative exploration where I reimagined an Elvive shampoo advertisement purely from my own vision, without any client request. Using Adobe Photoshop, I focused on advanced photo manipulation, lighting, and composition techniques to bring the concept to life.

The most challenging aspects were achieving realistic reflections and shadows, ensuring the bottle and surrounding elements felt naturally integrated within the scene. The precision required for light interaction, material texture adjustments, and depth perception made this a demanding yet rewarding process.

This piece took me [19 hours] to complete, pushing my Photoshop skills to new heights as I meticulously fine-tuned every detail to create a polished and high-impact visual.

The story

The idea for the Elvive Shampoo Manipulation Design came to me one evening when I was reflecting on how beauty product advertisements blend realism and creativity. I wanted to challenge myself by creating a high-end, visually striking shampoo ad, capturing the essence of luxury and elegance associated with brands like Elvive. The image in my mind was of a shampoo bottle surrounded by water droplets and soft lighting, all balanced with perfect reflections and shadows.

I began by sourcing a high-quality image of the Elvive bottle and isolated it, focusing on the key elements that would make it stand out. The first challenge was achieving the perfect reflections on the bottle’s glossy surface. I spent hours tweaking the light source to ensure it interacted with the bottle naturally, creating realistic highlights and reflections. This part required patience and a keen eye for detail to ensure the product looked believable.

Once the reflections were set, I worked on the shadows. Creating the right depth and balance between light and dark areas was essential for the design’s realism. The shadows had to complement the light source, so I spent time adjusting their softness and intensity. I also added background elements and textures, layering them carefully to build an atmosphere that complemented the product without distracting from the main focus.

After refining every detail—from the lighting and reflections to the water droplets and background textures—I stepped back to assess the final result. The piece felt polished and sophisticated, just as I had envisioned. This project was not only a test of my manipulation skills but also a creative journey, pushing me to explore new techniques and bring a simple product ad to life with a fresh perspective.

OUR APPROACH

In this design, we focused on blending realism with creativity, aiming to capture the luxury and elegance of Elvive shampoo. Through meticulous photo manipulation, we paid close attention to every detail—achieving perfect reflections, shadows, and lighting. Our approach involved carefully crafting each element, from the bottle's glossy surface to the surrounding atmosphere, ensuring everything felt naturally integrated. The result is a polished, high-end visual that not only highlights the product but also elevates it through thoughtful design.

Project Description

As the lead graphic designer for Mazag Coffee, I was tasked with developing a complete visual identity that would represent the essence of the brand—energizing, vibrant, and bold. The project included designing the logo, packaging, and promotional materials that would resonate with coffee lovers and reflect the premium quality of the product. The story

For this project, I created a dynamic visual for Mazag Coffee, placing the coffee package in the middle of a lush green jungle surrounded by playful squirrels. Using Adobe Photoshop, I blended high-quality jungle images with the coffee package, adjusting shadows and lighting to match the natural environment. I carefully positioned the squirrels to interact with the scene, adding energy and life to the composition. The final image was enhanced with light rays and color adjustments to evoke a sense of freshness and vitality, reflecting the invigorating essence of Mazag Coffee in a vibrant, natural setting.
